Daily Task Features

  • Add Daily Tasks: Easily log the work you've done each day.
  • Track Time: Record how long you spend on each task.
  • Task Status: Mark tasks as pending or completed to stay organized.
  • Work History: View past logs anytime to reflect and report.
  • Submit Reports: Instantly share your day’s work with your manager.
  • Dashboard Overview: See summaries of your weekly and monthly performance.
  • Daily Reminders: Get notified to complete and submit logs on time.
  • Secure Login: Every employee has a secure, private account.
  • File Attachments: Add screenshots, files, or documents with your tasks.

Why use WorkLog Pro? Because it makes it easy for you to stay productive and transparent. With just a few clicks each day, you can maintain a complete, accurate history of all your work — no stress, no hassle.
